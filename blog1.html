Why most websites use .com?

July 12, 2021

Introduction

Have u ever imagined that why most companies use .com as an extension. Lately, when Im surfing over internet I observed that mostly all websites use .com as an extension. Then some questions are popping on my mind that why .com ? why not other domains? Let us know about .com

Why companies prefer .com ?

  • Most people will assume that your website ends in .com
  • Owning a .com domain lends credibility to your brand
  • The .com has no TLD specific rules tied to it

Google loves .com domain

The .com domain extension is by far the most used and valuable domain suffix. Put bluntly, Google is biased towards websites with .com TLD.

Although businesses may wonder why .com is still afforded so much authority by Google. The answer is within human interaction with the search engine, that’s easy and familiar.

A website with the domain name yourbusiness.com is likely to be afforded more authority by Google than yourbusiness.co.uk or yourbusiness.business because users are more likely to type in yourbusiness.com than yourbusiness.co.uk or any other TDL for that matter.

Which is what leads many businesses to seek their domain name with a less popular suffix. This might seem reasonable for branding purposes could be detrimental to your site’s online visibility.

Pros and Cons

The Pros:

  • Memorability
  • Brandability
  • Appeals to Global Audience

The Cons:

  • .com Domains Are So Expensive.
  • Limited Availability.

Is there any Alternative..

A .net extension is a popular option that’s often recommended to buyers whenever the desired .com domain is taken. The .net stands for ‘network’ and was originally designed for umbrella websites that serve smaller sites, such as web hosting companies.

If the .com name you want isn’t available, find out if the current owner is in the same industry before settling for a .net. That’s because you don’t want your customers becoming there’s.

In general, the .net extension is trusted and perceived as authoritative by consumers. It’s also been around enough that people can easily remember it.

From the SEO end of things, .net is better than other specialty extensions. But since it isn’t quite up there with .com and .org extensions, you might not get the same benefits as using those alternatives. If your ideal .com name isn’t available, .net is typically a strong second choice.

However, .net isn’t a good option for your business in most cases. The "com" in the .com domain name indicates a “commercial” site. This can cover business websites, websites that want to make money online, personal websites, blogs, portfolios, and more

On the other hand, the “net” in the .net domain name extension stands for “network”. It was designed for the internet, networking, and email service providers.

The Bottom Line..

Having a website with a .com domain improves credibility and reliability. The .com domain is used for commercial businesses, even sartup companies can use this as it creates some brand .It makes easy for people to serach with .com as everyone thinks that a website ends with a .com domain. Google’s only reason to prioritize one extension over the other is that Internet users default to .com domains. In fact, more than 50 percent of all websites on the web use the .com extension.
